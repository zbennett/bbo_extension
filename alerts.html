Precision players should indicate that 1♥ and 1♠ are limited bids (“10-15 HCP” or as appropriate), in addition to providing information for the 1♣, ♦, 1NT, and 2♣ openings. In an ACBL open regional pairs or higher events. it is reasonable to assume opponents have a basic familiarity with Precision. This is not a reasonable assumption in Support Your Club games on BBO. Disclose.

Within ACBL-land, 1NT without an explanation is assumed to be 15-17 HCP. In wider circles, you should always indicate your notrump range and style, e.g. “15-17 HCP, stiff Q, K, A permitted.”
